## Image prompts

Use the same base style for every image:

> Dark editorial tech illustration, deep black background #050505, cyan #00D4FF and amber #FFB800 accent lighting, marble #F7F5F0 subtle highlights, minimalist composition, cinematic lighting, no text, no logos, high contrast, 16:9 aspect ratio, 4K detail.

### Scene 1 — Hook: Oracle vs Agent
Split-screen illustration. Left: a clean glowing chat bubble with soft cyan light on a black background. Right: a silhouetted founder sitting at a desk overflowing with paper receipts, invoices, and notification pop-ups, warm clutter contrasting with cold clean left side. Cinematic, high contrast, no text.

### Scene 2 — The Problem
A small business founder silhouette buried under floating documents, receipt papers, calendar alerts, and invoice envelopes in a dark void. One hand reaches up toward a cyan light above. Moody, editorial, high contrast, no text.

### Scene 3 — Hermes Needs a Home
A glowing cyan ring floating in the center of a dark void, with faint architectural columns of a Greek temple rising behind it like a wireframe. A small figure stands below looking up. Cinematic, minimal, no text.

### Scene 4 — The Seven Heroes
Seven abstract hero figures standing in a semicircle around a central glowing cyan orb. Each figure has a subtle color aura: blue, purple, indigo, green, amber, red, gold. Thin light lines connect them to the center and to each other. Dark background, cinematic, no text.

### Scene 5 — Honest Status Tags
Three floating glass-like cards in a dark void. First card glows amber, second glows cyan, third glows warm parchment white. Clean UI, minimal, cinematic lighting, no text.

### Scene 6 — Architecture Flow
Minimalist dark data-flow diagram. A small human figure on the left, a cyan glowing core in the center, and seven colored nodes branching to icons suggesting spreadsheet, credit card, lock, calendar, handshake, brain. Neon lines, black background, no text.

### Scene 7 — Install & Workshop
A laptop on a dark desk glowing with cyan terminal light, surrounded by three blurred silhouettes of people collaborating. In the background, a faint projection of a dashboard. Moody, cinematic, no text.

### Scene 8 — Mission: One Million
A vast dark landscape with a single glowing path of cyan light leading to a horizon. Small points of light multiply along the path like a network. One large amber light pulses in the distance. Cinematic, epic, no text.

### Scene 9 — Closing: Open Source Must Win
A simple, bold image: an open hand releasing a glowing cyan bird or light particle into a dark sky. Minimal, symbolic, hopeful, no text.
