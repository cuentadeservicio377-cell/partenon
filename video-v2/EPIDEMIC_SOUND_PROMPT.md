# Epidemic Sound Prompt — Partenon Hackathon Video v2

## Prompt to paste into Epidemic Sound AI / search

```
Cinematic ambient tech background track for a 2-minute AI/agent explainer video.
Dark, futuristic, minimal.
Sparse synth pads, subtle pulsing bass, soft arpeggios.
Slow build from quiet atmosphere to a gentle driving pulse at the midpoint.
No vocals, no lead melody, no acoustic instruments.
Mood: confident, mysterious, hopeful.
Tempo around 85–95 BPM.
Ends with a clean resolve, not an abrupt cut.
```

## Alternative keyword searches

If the AI prompt does not give good results, try searching with these terms:

- `ambient cinematic tech`
- `minimal synth underscore`
- `future noir ambient`
- `dark electronic atmosphere`
- `sci-fi documentary background`
- `corporate technology minimal`

## What to avoid

- Anything with vocals, whistling, or humming.
- Busy drums or percussion in the first 30 seconds.
- Major-key happy corporate music.
- Tracks that fade out (we need a clean ending or an editable loop).

## Technical specs

- Format: WAV or 320 kbps MP3.
- Duration: at least 2:30, or a seamless loop.
- Target loudness: around -14 LUFS so it sits under narration without ducking too hard.
- Save the final file as: `video-v2/assets/bgm.mp3`
