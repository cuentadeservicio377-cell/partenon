# Pitch Deck Template

Use this structure when building a slide deck with `presentation_builder.py`.

## Slides

1. **Title**
   - Headline: the promise in one line.
   - Subheadline: who it is for.

2. **Problem**
   - The specific pain the audience faces.
   - Cost of not solving it.

3. **Solution**
   - What you sell in one sentence.
   - Key benefit.

4. **How It Works**
   - 3-step process.
   - Keep it concrete.

5. **Proof**
   - Metrics, testimonials, or case studies.
   - No unverified claims.

6. **Pricing / Next Step**
   - Clear offer or call to action.

7. **Thank You / Contact**
   - One action, one contact method.

## Design Notes

- One idea per slide.
- Use numbers when available.
- Avoid filler words and generic adjectives.
- Follow the brand .design file for colors and typography.
