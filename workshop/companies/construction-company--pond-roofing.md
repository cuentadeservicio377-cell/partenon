## Real pain points from public sources
1. **Project coordination across crews, materials, and weather**. Roofing is weather-dependent. Scheduling jobs, confirming material deliveries, and keeping homeowners informed requires many touchpoints.
2. **Permit and insurance paperwork**. Each job may involve permits, homeowner association approvals, and insurance documentation. Missing one document can delay a project.
3. **Customer communication at scale**. With thousands of jobs, keeping homeowners updated with photos, schedules, and answers is labor-intensive. Reviews consistently praise communication, which means the company invests heavily in it.
4. **Supplier and subcontractor relationships**. The company works with multiple manufacturers and trades (windows, gutters, siding). Coordinating warranties, deliveries, and quality standards is complex.
5. **Long-term reputation management**. 60+ years of family ownership means the brand is tied to trust. One badly managed project or credential lapse can damage decades of goodwill.

## Why Partenon fits
- **Strategist** can build phase checklists for each job type (roof, siding, windows) and assign owners and due dates.
- **Diplomat** can track homeowner milestones, insurance adjusters, and supplier contacts with confirmed-in-writing records.
- **Scribe** can cost each project separately (materials, labor, permits, commissions) and compare it to the estimate.
- **Guardian** can manage credentials for manufacturer certifications, insurance, bonding, and shared tool accounts.
- **Herald** can generate review-request sequences and local SEO content.
- **Collector** can collect deposits and final payments through Stripe or ACH and reconcile them against projects.
