## Real Pain Points (from public information and retail industry data)

1. **Inventory accuracy across channels.** K&L updates inventory in real time on its website and operates three stores. A 35% SKU-level error rate is common in retail without professional inventory programs, leading to phantom availability or missed sales.
2. **Stockouts and overstock.** Wine is seasonal and release-driven; overbuying ties up working capital, while stockouts of allocated or trending bottles lose loyal customers.
3. **Supplier and distributor relationships.** Allocation lists, payment terms, and delivery windows with distributors must be tracked closely, especially for limited releases.
4. **Payment diversity.** In-store card, online checkout, phone orders, and corporate accounts create reconciliation work and fraud exposure.
5. **Staff knowledge dependency.** Customers rely on staff recommendations; training and consistent product knowledge across three locations is operationally expensive.

## Why Partenon Fits

- **Scribe** can track COGS, shipping, and payment fees by product line and store, producing a live margin dashboard.
- **Collector** can handle Stripe checkout, subscriptions (wine clubs), refunds, and failed-payment retries across channels.
- **Strategist** can coordinate restocking, promotional calendars, and staff training schedules.
- **Diplomat** can manage distributor relationships, allocation milestones, and corporate-account follow-ups.
- **Herald** can generate the monthly newsletter, social content around new arrivals, and local event calendars.
- **Brain** can index buying decisions and customer preferences so recommendations stay consistent across locations.
