## Real Pain Points (from public website and industry context)

1. **Project coordination across a remote team.** The agency is fully remote and delivers multi-week Strategy First engagements plus ongoing Marketing Operating System retainers, so tasks, deadlines, and handoffs must be tightly coordinated.
2. **Cash flow from retainers and fixed-scope engagements.** Strategy First starts at $10,000 as a fixed-scope project; retainers run $5K-$10K/month. Late or inconsistent client payments directly impact operations.
3. **Proposal-to-delivery handoffs.** A 40-60 page strategy document must translate into an executable 90-day calendar, which often loses fidelity when passed from strategist to implementation team.
4. **Margin pressure on services.** With salary inflation and client fee pressure across the agency sector, utilization and project profitability must be tracked closely.
5. **Client milestone management.** Multiple clients at different stages (diagnostic, strategy, ongoing retainer) need follow-ups, contract renewals, and scope-change logging.

## Why Partenon Fits

- **Strategist** can manage Strategy First projects, assign owners to each deliverable, and generate weekly planning/retro briefings.
- **Diplomat** can log every client interaction, track proposal milestones, and run daily follow-up reports so no renewal slips.
- **Scribe** can match project budgets to actual hours and vendor costs (writers, designers, ad spend) and flag overruns above 10%.
- **Herald** can apply its own brand-voice rules to produce the agency's own content calendar, case studies, and newsletter drafts.
- **Collector** can invoice retainers and follow up on overdue accounts automatically.
- **Brain** can index positioning decisions and winning proposal templates so new consultants onboard faster.
