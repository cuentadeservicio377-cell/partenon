# Company Card: Outseta

**Business type:** Bootstrapped SaaS / membership operating system  
**Location:** Boston, MA, USA; distributed team  
**Founded:** 2017  
**Founders:** Geoff Roberts, Dave McGraw, Joe Mercurio  
**Approximate size:** Small self-managed team; serves 6,000+ companies  
**Website:** https://www.outseta.com  

---

## Services and products

- All-in-one platform: CRM, subscription billing, email marketing, help desk, user authentication
- Targets bootstrapped SaaS, paid newsletters, online courses, and membership communities
- Integrates with Stripe, Webflow, Framer, Carrd, Squarespace, Zapier, and Make
- Pricing tiers from $47/month (Founder) to $497/month (50K), plus 1-2% transaction fees

---

## Rough revenue estimate

Private, bootstrapped company. Pricing and transaction-fee structure suggest revenue scales with customer count and payment volume rather than large enterprise contracts.

---

## Real pain points (from public sources)

1. **Transaction-fee stack.** Outseta charges 1-2% on top of Stripe's standard 2.9% + 30c. For a $20/month membership, combined fees materially reduce net revenue.
2. **Feature depth trade-offs.** Reviews note that individual features are not as advanced as specialized tools and customization is limited compared to best-of-breed solutions.
3. **No native mobile operator app.** Users mention a missing iOS app and a desire for a more flexible dashboard with deeper analytics.
4. **Platform complexity for very early startups.** Some buyers find the all-in-one scope overwhelming before they have product/market fit.
5. **Trial-conversion optimization gap.** Outseta handles billing and email but is not specialized for trial-to-paid conversion optimization, which is a distinct growth discipline.
