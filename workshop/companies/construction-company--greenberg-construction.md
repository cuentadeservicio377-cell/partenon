## Real Pain Points (from public reviews and industry context)

1. **Subcontractor coordination.** Construction projects rely on multiple trades (framing, electrical, plumbing, drywall). Reviews of similar firms highlight that poor communication between subcontracted teams causes repairs and completions to drag out.
2. **Schedule delays.** Customer reviews in the construction category frequently cite projects stretching weeks or months beyond original timelines due to coordination gaps and material lead times.
3. **Change-order and cost tracking.** Design-build work involves ongoing scope decisions; without real-time cost tracking, change orders erode margin before the project closes.
4. **Payment timing and cash flow.** Construction payment cycles are notoriously slow; subcontractors and suppliers must be paid before client retainers and progress payments arrive.
5. **Document and decision scattering.** Material selections, permits, site photos, and client approvals live in email, text messages, and showroom visits, making it hard to reconstruct project status.

## Why Partenon Fits

- **Strategist** can create phase checklists for each project (pre-construction, construction, closeout) and assign owners and due dates.
- **Diplomat** can track supplier and subcontractor contracts, log milestones, and schedule follow-ups before materials are needed on site.
- **Scribe** can cost each project separately (labor, materials, subcontractors, permits) and compare actual spend to the estimate weekly.
- **Guardian** can manage shared accounts for supplier portals, CAD/modeling tools, and insurance systems.
- **Collector** can invoice progress payments and retainers, then follow up on overdue accounts.
- **Brain** can index lessons learned per project type (ADU, kitchen, whole-home) so estimates improve over time.
