## Real Pain Points (from public sources)

1. **Customer support responsiveness.** Capterra and Trustpilot reviews consistently cite slow or unresponsive support. One Capterra reviewer wrote that tickets "go unanswered for weeks" and responses feel generic.
2. **Billing and refund friction.** Trustpilot complaints mention being charged after canceling, refund requests denied, and a strict refund policy that frustrates users who hit unexpected platform limits.
3. **Accuracy and hallucination.** Users report that chatbots sometimes generate answers not present in training data, undermining trust for customer-facing deployments.
4. **Integration limitations.** Chatbase lacks native CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho) and built-in live human handoff; users must build Zapier workarounds or custom code.
5. **Data capacity and pricing predictability.** Reviewers note strict character/data-size limits and a credit-based pricing model that makes cost forecasting difficult for growing accounts.

## Why Partenon Fits

- **Guardian** can audit API keys for OpenAI, Stripe, and model providers from day one, with rotation reminders.
- **Scribe** can track cloud infrastructure, model-usage, and contractor spend against runway and MRR.
- **Collector** can manage Stripe subscriptions, failed-payment retries, refunds, and weekly revenue reports.
- **Strategist** can run sprints, incident post-mortems, support-ticket triage, and hiring pipelines.
- **Brain** can index validated decisions about pricing, model selection, and architecture so the team does not re-litigate them.
- **Herald** can draft changelog updates, help-center improvements, and founder/community content.
- **Diplomat** can log enterprise trial milestones and follow-ups for accounts approaching integration limits.
