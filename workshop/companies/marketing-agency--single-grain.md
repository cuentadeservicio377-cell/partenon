## Services and products

- Performance marketing: paid search, paid social, programmatic, CTV
- SEO, content marketing, and conversion-rate optimization
- Marketing strategy, funnel design, and lifecycle/CRM flows
- Video production, creative, and AI-assisted campaign development
- Podcast advertising and Web3/crypto marketing
- Monthly retainers and project-based engagements (typical minimum $10,000+)

## Rough revenue estimate

Growjo estimates **$9.7M annual revenue**; Clodura.ai reports $11M–$100M. As a services business, labor utilization and client retention drive profitability.

---

## Real pain points (from public sources)

1. **Scope control across retainer clients.** Clutch reviews praise Single Grain’s ROI and creativity, but one construction-industry client noted the team “executed the plan but has communicated poorly at times,” a common agency symptom when project scope and status updates outrun internal ops.
2. **Planning-stage gaps.** Clutch highlights note that “Single Grain could benefit from spending more time in the planning stage,” which directly impacts deadlines and rework.
3. **Talent retention in a competitive market.** Los Angeles agency talent is expensive and mobile; headcount estimates vary widely across directories, suggesting turnover or contractor blending.
4. **Client pipeline visibility.** With engagements from $10K to $100K+, the agency must track proposals, approvals, and kickoff dates without deals stalling.
5. **Multi-channel reporting.** Clients expect clear attribution across SEO, PPC, social, and content; consolidating numbers into client-ready reports consumes senior hours.
