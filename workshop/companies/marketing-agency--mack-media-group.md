# Company Card: Mack Media Group

**Business type:** Digital marketing agency serving local/SMB clients  
**Location:** Brookfield, CT, USA (also Pensacola, FL)  
**Founded:** Started by owner Scott Johnson after being disappointed with previous agency results  
**Approximate size:** Small agency; 46 Glassdoor reviews, 86% would recommend to a friend  
**Website:** https://mackmediagroup.com  

---

## Services and products

- Local SEO and content marketing
- Paid search / paid social (Bing Ads certified)
- Website design and conversion optimization
- Social media and reputation management
- Monthly retainers and project-based engagements, often starting with a free quote/audit

---

## Rough revenue estimate

Private company. Revenue is retainer-driven and depends on client count, average contract value, and utilization. As a services business, labor cost and client retention drive profitability.

---

## Real pain points (from public sources)

1. **Internal management and training gaps.** Glassdoor reviews cite "poor management, lack of training, unclear expectations, toxic communication, and delayed final pay." These operational issues increase turnover risk and reduce service consistency.
2. **Scope control across retainer clients.** The founder story is built on disappointment with other agencies. Without tight scope tracking, agencies over-service clients and erode margin.
3. **Pipeline and follow-up visibility.** With dozens of clients and weekly progress emails, proposals, kickoffs, and renewals can slip through the cracks without a shared CRM.
4. **Multi-channel reporting.** Clients expect clear attribution across SEO, PPC, and social. Consolidating numbers into client-ready reports consumes senior hours.
5. **Talent retention in a competitive market.** Negative employee feedback makes hiring harder; a small agency relies on a few key people.
