## Real Pain Points (from public reviews and industry coverage)

1. **Long wait times during peak hours.** Multiple location reviews note that lines and drink preparation times can frustrate customers, especially when each cup is hand-crafted to order.
2. **Inconsistent service speed and quality.** Review summaries flag occasional complaints about drink preparation and staff interactions, suggesting operational variance across locations.
3. **Pricing pressure.** Customers mention upcharges for non-dairy milk and smaller-than-expected food portions, which can erode perceived value and repeat visits.
4. **Labor cost intensity.** The made-to-order model requires trained baristas at every location, making labor scheduling and payroll visibility critical to margin.
5. **Cashless payments.** The chain operates cashless, which simplifies reconciliation but can exclude cash-preferred customers and requires reliable card/QR payment infrastructure.

## Why Partenon Fits

- **Scribe** can parse daily POS exports and supplier invoices, splitting costs into fixed (rent, base labor) and variable (beans, milk, packaging) buckets so the owner sees true margin per store.
- **Strategist** can build staff scheduling checklists and morning-briefing routines tied to expected traffic, reducing wait-time spikes.
- **Herald** can generate local Instagram/Reels calendars and geo-targeted copy promoting mobile order and off-peak visits.
- **Collector** can create Stripe payment links for catering orders and loyalty subscriptions without building a custom checkout.
- **Diplomat** can track vendor relationships (roasters, bakery suppliers) and contract renewal milestones.
