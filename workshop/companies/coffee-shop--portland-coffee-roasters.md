## Real Pain Points (from public sources)

1. **Packaging sustainability trade-offs.** A 2019 Sprudge interview notes that Portland Coffee Roasters' new bags were not recyclable or compostable at that time because lifecycle analysis showed many "greener" materials carried hidden greenhouse-gas or landfill trade-offs. Balancing freshness, cost, and sustainability claims is an ongoing operational and marketing tension.
2. **Multi-channel inventory complexity.** The company sells through its own cafes, grocery chains (Fred Meyer, New Seasons, QFC, Safeway), Amazon, and food-service accounts. Each channel has different margin, delivery cadence, and packaging needs.
3. **Carbon-neutral operations cost tracking.** Portland Coffee Roasters has maintained carbon-neutral operations since 2008, composts up to 1,000 lb of organic waste per month, and publishes a sustainability report. Tracking the true cost of these programs against product margins requires disciplined finance data.
4. **Supplier and farmer relationship management.** Direct-trade and sustainability-focused sourcing means long lead times, contract negotiations with importers, and follow-up with origin partners.
5. **Local marketing consistency.** With retail, wholesale, and online channels, the team must coordinate local events, new roast drops, and sustainability storytelling without a dedicated marketing department.

## Why Partenon Fits

- **Scribe** can track COGS, packaging costs, and sustainability-program spend by channel (cafe, wholesale, grocery, online) and flag low-margin lines.
- **Strategist** can schedule roast batches, cafe prep, roastery deliveries, and staff shifts in one calendar.
- **Herald** can generate local social calendars around new roast drops, sustainability reports, and neighborhood events.
- **Collector** can set up Stripe payment links for subscriptions, catering, and online orders, plus wholesale invoicing.
- **Diplomat** can log green-coffee importer contracts, delivery milestones, and farmer partnership follow-ups.
- **Guardian** can secure shared logins for POS, accounting, and e-commerce platforms.
